Introduction

My journey began in January 2023 when I joined the company as a BI Developer, specializing in crafting cutting-edge BI solutions leveraging Microsoft PowerBI.
Throughout my tenure, I embraced opportunities to expand my technical repertoire, delving into the realms of SQL and Python.
This pursuit of knowledge propelled me along a transformative path, evolving from a BI Developer to a proficient Data Warehouse Developer, and ultimately assuming the role of a Data Engineer.

As a Data Engineer, I'm immersed in projects of considerable scale, where my primary responsibilities revolve around maintaining robust Data Architecture. My daily tasks span a broad spectrum, including monitoring data flows, managing ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) and ELT (Extract, Load, Transform) processes, implementing data transformations and normalizations, overseeing data replications, and driving automation initiatives, among others. In essence, I'm the engine behind the scenes, ensuring that data moves seamlessly through our systems, is transformed accurately, and is readily accessible for analysis and decision-making. It's a dynamic role that demands a meticulous approach and a knack for problem-solving.

Experience

In 2020, after spending five enriching years in Northern Ireland, I returned to Slovakia and joined the Amazon fulfillment center in Sered. During my tenure, I evolved from various roles, culminating in my position as a Learning and Development Trainer and Analyst.
My journey into data analysis began with a keen focus on data-related tasks, providing me with unique insights beyond the surface level. This deep dive sparked my interest in analysis, leading me to master Excel and subsequently develop automated solutions for the entire team using VBA.
Driven by a vision of enhanced data readability, I ventured into the realm of Business Intelligence (BI). It was here that I encountered Power BI and DAX, tools that opened up new dimensions of data analysis for me. From that point forward, I proudly served as the team's official Data Analyst.
